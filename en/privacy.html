Privacy Policy

This page describes how the NAT Online Check handles information.

1. Data We Process

  • The tool uses WebRTC and public STUN servers in your browser to retrieve mapping info.
  • No account is required. We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • By default, results are not stored on any server.

2. How It Is Used

  • Results are displayed locally in the page.
  • You may copy results manually via your browser.

3. Third-party Services

  • The test contacts public STUN servers (e.g., Google, Cloudflare, Twilio). They may log basic network metadata per their policies.
  • If ads or analytics are enabled in the future, this page will be updated accordingly.

4. Cookies & Consent

This site does not set cookies by default. If ads/analytics are enabled, consent mechanisms will be provided where required.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page or email support@example.com.
